THE MOBI

The MOBI Current provides excess of 1,200 nutritious meals per month to chilldren under 15 living in squatters camps in Hatcliffe.We believe that the most effective way of helping other is to understand their plight their trust before solution into onto them. The best way to achieve this is to spend time within specific communities, developing relationship with assisting where we can. We strongly believe that, through the use of MOBI, we be able to develop this relationship and provide long term assistance to the people that we visit.

The Mobi is also equipped to react to crises involving at risk communities and provide disaster relief where needed and is a fixture at all Harare's fairs,exhibitions and markets where it is used as a platform to raise awareness,fundraise and sell crafts,curios and artwork on behalf of our vulnerable communities.
